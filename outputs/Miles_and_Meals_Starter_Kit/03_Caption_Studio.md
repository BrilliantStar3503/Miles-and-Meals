# Caption Studio

## Brand Voice

Miles & Meals captions should sound:

- Observant.
- Warm.
- Specific.
- Premium without being stiff.
- Curious without exaggerating.
- Personal without oversharing.

Avoid:

- Fake travel authority.
- Generic wanderlust language.
- Overclaiming.
- Captions that describe things not visible or experienced.

## Caption Template

```text
Opening sensory detail or honest moment.

Short destination or food context.

Personal reflection, travel note, or discovery.

Soft call to action.
```

## Instagram Caption Prompt

```text
Create 3 Instagram caption options for Miles & Meals.

Brand voice: premium, natural, cinematic, elegant, authentic.
Content type: [photo/reel/carousel]
Destination: [city, country]
Specific location: [place]
Food or attraction: [subject]
Story notes: [what actually happened]
Mood: [calm/adventurous/romantic/curious/cozy]
Preset style: [Alpine Swiss/Tropical Paradise/Emerald Forest/Golden Escape/City Explorer/Cafe Stories/Night Lights]
CTA: [save/share/comment/follow/none]

Rules:
- Do not invent facts.
- Keep it authentic.
- Mention concrete sensory details when provided.
- Include one concise option, one storytelling option, and one poetic option.
```
