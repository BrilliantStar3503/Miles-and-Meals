# Publishing Approval Checklist

Use this before scheduling or publishing any Miles & Meals post.

## Authenticity

- [ ] Destination is real and accurately represented.
- [ ] Location names are correct.
- [ ] Food, landmarks, people, and scenery are not misrepresented.
- [ ] Caption does not invent facts or experiences.
- [ ] Any uncertain claim is removed or softened.

## Visual Quality

- [ ] Image or video feels natural, not AI-generated.
- [ ] Facial identity is preserved.
- [ ] Scenery is preserved.
- [ ] Highlights and shadows are controlled.
- [ ] Noise reduction does not smear detail.
- [ ] Sharpening is clean and not crunchy.
- [ ] Crop works for the target platform.

## Brand Fit

- [ ] Content feels premium, natural, bright, cinematic, elegant, and authentic.
- [ ] Color treatment matches the selected preset family.
- [ ] Story aligns with Miles & Meals content pillars.
- [ ] Post is recognizable as part of the brand.

## Platform Readiness

- [ ] Caption is approved.
- [ ] Hashtags are reviewed.
- [ ] ALT text is written.
- [ ] Location tag is correct.
- [ ] Cover frame or thumbnail is selected.
- [ ] Audio is cleared or platform-safe.
- [ ] Export settings match the platform.

## Final Decision

- [ ] Approved by human creative director.
- [ ] Scheduled or published manually.
- [ ] Analytics tracking row created.
