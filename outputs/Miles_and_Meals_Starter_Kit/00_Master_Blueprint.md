# Miles & Meals - Master Blueprint v1.0

## Vision

Build Miles & Meals into a premium travel brand and AI-powered content production ecosystem that combines authentic storytelling with intelligent automation.

The goal is not only to create beautiful travel content. The goal is to build a repeatable production system that can scale across hundreds or thousands of photos, videos, destinations, captions, posts, and analytics records while preserving a consistent visual identity and high creative standards.

AI handles repetitive production work. Human creative direction remains in control.

## Mission

Produce world-class travel content that inspires people to explore destinations through authentic experiences, cinematic visuals, meaningful storytelling, and local food discoveries.

Every piece of content should reflect quality, consistency, and authenticity.

## Core Principles

### Authenticity First

- Never fabricate destinations.
- Never misrepresent locations.
- Preserve reality while enhancing beauty.
- Keep food, scenery, people, and context truthful.

### Natural AI Enhancement

AI improves quality without making content look artificial.

Enhancements should:

- Preserve facial identity.
- Preserve scenery and architecture.
- Improve detail and clarity.
- Reduce noise.
- Recover highlights and shadows.
- Remove haze naturally.
- Upscale only when useful.
- Maintain realism.

### Human Creativity

Creative decisions remain manual, including:

- Selecting photos and videos.
- Lightroom adjustments.
- CapCut editing.
- Storytelling.
- Captions.
- Publishing approval.
- Brand voice and creative direction.

### Automation

Everything repetitive should eventually become automated, especially file organization, naming, metadata, captions, hashtag drafts, publishing drafts, analytics tracking, and archive maintenance.
